Event Description

Mr. & Miss NIT transcends beyond a conventional beauty pageant - it's a platform that celebrates personality, intelligence, and charisma. This prestigious competition brings together the most talented individuals from our institute to showcase their unique qualities.

Contestants will navigate through multiple challenging rounds including professional ramp walks, creative talent showcases, and thought-provoking Q&A sessions. The competition is designed to test not just appearance, but confidence, communication skills, and overall persona.

Do you have what it takes to represent the spirit of NIT?

Judging Criteria

  • Confidence & Stage Presence

    Command of the stage and overall confidence

  • Attire & Grooming

    Style, presentation, and attention to detail

  • Communication Skills

    Articulation and clarity of expression

  • Talent & Creativity

    Originality and skill in talent presentation

  • Intelligence & Wit

    Quick thinking and intelligent responses
